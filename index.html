A Peek Into Our Love Story ❤️

It all started in a quiet corner of corporate life. Was it love at first sight? Nah. But did it feel like every moment, every coffee break, every shared laugh was leading us here? Absolutely. It was the kind of connection that felt less like a spark and more like a warm, comforting truth—it was always meant to be.

Meet the dynamic duo: A rugged Haryanvi Chhorra and a graceful Kannadathi. Yes, we know, it sounds a little bit like the sequel to 2 States, but this one is ours—and it's so much sweeter! We are the living proof that a shared table of Jowar Roti and Saag alongside Masala Dosa and Filter Coffee is the perfect recipe for forever.

Join us as we prove that love truly knows no boundaries—and that our blend of North and South is the most delicious one yet!
